Dat Nguyen

Distributed Systems | Erlang/OTP | IoT/Telecom | Cloud Infra

My name is Dat Nguyen, I’m an engineer with 10+ years of experience building fault-tolerant, distributed systems using Erlang/OTP in cloud-native environments. I am passionate about applying functional programming and resilient architecture to high-availability platforms supporting real-time communication, automation, and telemetry.

I’ve contributed to telecom infrastructure (Ericsson), network automation platforms (Cisco ConfD/NSO), and large-scale communication services (Voice, IVR, WhatsApp, SMS, USSD) at PxD. At 5GenCare, I design and operate real-time IoT/AIoT,high-availability backends focused on device communication and data processing.

Driven by a passion for functional programming and resilient architecture, I focus on building high-availability systems that are scalable, maintainable, and built to last. I also actively contribute to the Erlang/OTP ecosystem.

I hold a Master’s degree in Information Technology from the University of Information Technology, Vietnam, with IEEE-published research on hybrid geolocation using LoRaWAN and Semtech LR1110.

selected publications

  1. M.Sc. Thesis
    Hybrid Geolocation Gateway with LoRaWAN and Semtech LR1110: Real-time GNSS/WiFi/LoRa Integration
    Dat Tran Tien Nguyen
    University of Information Technology, Vietnam, May 2022
    Abs DOI Bib HTML PDF

    This thesis presents the design and implementation of a hybrid geolocation gateway that integrates LoRaWAN technology with Semtech LR1110 chip for real-time GNSS/WiFi/LoRa positioning. The system demonstrates improved accuracy and reliability in challenging urban environments through multi-source location fusion algorithms.
